What is Virtual Staging?

Virtual staging uses digital technology to add realistic furniture, décor, and design elements to photos of empty or outdated spaces. It helps buyers visualize a home's potential and increases online engagement — often leading to more showings and a faster sale.

You can show these virtual staging photos directly on the MLS listing for your property sale.

Answers about home staging costs, coverage across Toronto & the GTA, and how we keep our rates affordable.

How much does home staging cost in Toronto?

Most Toronto staging companies charge $2,000–$10,000. NH Staging & Renovations offers flat-rate packages at an affordable price: Partial Staging from $999 (living room & kitchen, washrooms, master bedroom, décor & furniture) and Full Staging from $1,999 (all bedrooms and foyer included). Virtual staging starts at $299.

Do you offer affordable home staging across the GTA?

Yes. We provide some of the best staging rates in the Greater Toronto Area, serving Toronto, North York, Scarborough, Etobicoke, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Richmond Hill and Markham — with a free first consultation on every package.

Do you provide home décor for resale houses?

Yes. We style resale houses with accent furniture, wall art, rugs, lighting and accessories so listings photograph beautifully and appeal to buyers — staged homes typically sell faster and for more.

What is virtual staging and how much does it cost?

Virtual staging digitally furnishes photos of your empty rooms with photorealistic furniture and décor. We deliver MLS-ready images within 48 hours, starting at $299.

Why choose NH Staging as your home staging company in Toronto?

Transparent flat-rate pricing, 48-hour average turnaround, 100+ projects completed, a 5-star client rating and limited-time package rates make NH Staging one of the most affordable home staging companies in Toronto and the GTA.
